Lunch

Adobo
  • 500g chicken or pork
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup vinegar
  • 1/2 cup water
  • 4 cloves garlic (crushed)
  • 2 bay leaves (optional but recommended)
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
Procedure

To cook simple adobo, combine the meat, soy sauce, vinegar, water, garlic, bay leaves, and pepper in a pot. Bring the mixture to a boil without stirring so the vinegar can cook properly. Once it boils, lower the heat and let it simmer until the meat becomes tenderâ€”about 25 to 30 minutes for chicken or 40 to 50 minutes for pork. Taste the sauce and adjust the seasoning with a bit of salt or soy sauce if needed. Continue simmering uncovered until the sauce reduces and thickens to your desired consistency. Once done, serve hot with rice.
